METHODOLOGY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

RELIABILITY CORRECTION (CRITICAL):
- Effective Availability: 88.2% = 90% efficiency √ó 98% reliability
- Effective Capacity: 4,191.3 min/week per machine (was 4,752 without reliability)
- Impact: Requires 432 machines instead of 366 (+18% increase)

LAYOUT STRATEGY:
- Compact vertical stacking (prefer taller grids with more rows)
- GAP = 0 ft (blocks touch directly, no spacing)
- Left-to-right process flow
- Overlap applied per equipment group specifications

FLOW CALCULATION:
- Center-to-center Euclidean distance between consecutive processes
- Total flow = Weekly_Demand √ó Distance_Per_Unit
- Example: P1 = 20,961.54 units √ó 229.91 ft = 4,819,267 unit-ft/week
