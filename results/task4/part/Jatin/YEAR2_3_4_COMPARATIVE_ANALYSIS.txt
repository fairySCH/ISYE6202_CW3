• Allows for smooth scaling without major facility redesigns

• Year 3 → Year 4: +1 machine (+1.3%)
  - Machines added: B1(+1)
• Total growth Year 2 → Year 4: +5 machines (+6.9%)

• Year 3: B1 becomes bottleneck at 101.6% utilization - indicates over-capacity
• Year 4: High utilization across board (98.1% average)
  - All areas except I operate near capacity (99%)
  - I area has slack (92.8%)

CAPACITY PLANNING OPPORTUNITY:
• Year 3 B1 bottleneck suggests potential for process improvement or additional 
  equipment in that area
• Year 4 shows efficient utilization for P1, indicating good capacity planning
